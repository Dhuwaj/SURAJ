Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

How do I install SURAJ?

Download the version for your device from our Downloads page. Follow the on-screen instructions to complete installation.

Why is my device not connecting?

Ensure there is wireless or wired connection to your device and your device is powered on. Restart the app if the issue persists.

Does SURAJ support solar energy monitoring?

Yes. SURAJ can track solar panels, inverters, wind microturbines, and other renewable systems.

How do I track my energy usage?

Open the Dashboard in the app to view real-time and historical consumption data.
